Lori Russell:
Westland Center Director
Lori started her career at Creative Child Care in 1994 and was promoted to the center location with the team work of very capable Assistant Directors at both locations.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Studies from the Ohio Dominican University and an Associate Degree of Applied Sciences in Early Childhood Education from Columbus State Community College. Lori has a National Administrator Credential from National Early Childhood Program Accreditation (NECPA) and is a member of the National Association for the Education of Young Children (NAEYC).
